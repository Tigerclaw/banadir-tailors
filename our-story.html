Welcome to the

BANADIR TAILOR

SINCE 1981

We offer a start to finish service of producing the highest quality unique bespoke line up of clothing.

Encouraged by his grandfather and father, Abubakar Ahmed learned to sew at a young age and spent many hours pedalling away on an industrial Singer sewing machine (which is still used in the business today) making little bags and troussers. He soon progressed to making clothes and formalised his early acquired skills training in Tailoring.

ABOUT OUR TAILOR

" Quality Tailoring services from well trained, professional tailors, tailoring to specific needs.

We are Banadir Tailor. A North West London tailors serving our local community.We have a combined experience of over 30 years providing clothing repairs, design and alterations. We offer a start to finish service of producing the highest quality unique bespoke line up of clothing. A truley World Class Service!

We will work closely with you to ensure you have the perfect fit every time, whether you need us for alterations, bespoke tailoring or to

create something for your wedding day.

We always begin with the client first by listening to your needs; we are then better informed to offer our knowledge and expertise to create the perfect garments for you.
